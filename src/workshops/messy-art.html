A workshop about turning messy feelings into messy art. You'll learn some ways to get out of your head and make expressive art, and we'll explore journaling as both a self-care activity and a way to preserve memories. The goal is to stop overthinking and make art that begins in the body instead of the brain.

I'll provide the art supplies and the space to get messy. You'll be provided with a journal of your own to take home to continue your journaling practice!

Wine, Beer, and N/A drinks will be available for purchase.
